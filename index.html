Welcome to PhrAIsier

How to Play

  1. Each day, a phrase will create an AI generated image
  2. You’ll be shown the image, how many words are in the phrase, and have 5 guesses to figure it out what the phrase is
  3. Correct guesses don’t count against you and words like: "a", "and", "the", and "in" are pre-filled
  4. After each guess, color coded words will indicate how close your guess was
    1. Green: The word is in the phrase
    2. Yellow: The word is close (think “stormy” vs. “storm” or “fire” vs. “bonfire”)
    3. Strikethrough: The word is not in the phrase, and you lose one guess
