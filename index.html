Welcome to PhrAIsier

How to Play

  1. Each day, a phrase will create an AI generated image
  2. You’ll be shown the image, how many words are in the phrase, and have 5 guesses to figure it out. Correct guesses don’t count against you (words like a, and, the, in are pre-filled)
  3. After each guess, color coded words will indicate how close your guess was
    1. Green: The word is correct and in the right place
    2. Yellow: The word is close (think “stormy” vs. “storm” or “Foggy” vs. “Fog”)
    3. Strikethrough: The word is not in the phrase
